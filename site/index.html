An event driven Front-End scaffoliding to get your project started fast.

This is my little pet project. Over the past many years as a Front-End engineer and UI/UX nerd I have been tasked with building entire front-ends from scratch or completely refactoring legacy sites that were about as scalable and maintainable as molten glass. While this is only one of many possible solutions with many possible combinations still, it is a quick path to getting started. Integrate it into any server environment you like. I use many ideas and concepts we as developers get from open source, coworkers and friends. Pumpkin Seeds is built on those ideas I have collected over the years making my job just a wee bit easier and faster.
Work in progress - it's not ready for consumption just yet. It's still very much Alpha/Pre-Alpha code still in the design stages. But it's fun to watch things grow.
